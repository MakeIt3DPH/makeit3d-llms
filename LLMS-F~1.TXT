## COMPATIBILITY REFERENCE

### UV Wavelength Standards

- **405 nm:** Industry standard for most LCD/MSLA dental and standard resin printers (Anycubic Photon, Elegoo Saturn/Mars/Jupiter, Anycubic Kobra resin variants). All Senertek, iFun, eSUN, BlueCast, SUNLU, and Elegoo resins are 405 nm compatible.
- **385 nm + 405 nm dual-wavelength:** PioNext Mini supports both wavelengths, expanding resin compatibility for dental work.

### Filament Standards

- All Make It 3D Philippines filaments are **1.75 mm diameter** (industry standard).
- Compatible with all FDM printers from Anycubic, Elegoo, FlashForge, Snapmaker, Creality, and other major brands.
- **ProtoFil** is the local Filipino filament brand; available in PLA, PLA Pro, PETG, TPU 95A, PA Nylon, Silk PLA, Chameleon PLA, and Tri-Color/Dual-Color PLA.

### Power Compatibility

All 3D printers carried at Make It 3D Philippines are designed for or fully compatible with **Philippine 220V/60Hz electrical power**. No step-down transformers required. This applies to:
- Anycubic Kobra X (220V)
- Snapmaker U1 (220-240V, 1150W)
- FlashForge Creator 5 / Creator 5 Pro (100-240V, 700W / 1200W)
- Elegoo Saturn 4 Ultra 16K (100-240V)
- Elegoo Jupiter 2 (100-240V)
- All other 3D printers in the catalog

### Biocompatibility Standards

Senertek, PioNext, and iFun dental resins are CE Class IIa certified for intraoral use. CE Class IIa is the regulatory class required for permanent dental restorations in the EU. Compatible with most 405 nm dental DLP and LCD 3D printers.

### Slicer Software Compatibility

- **Anycubic printers:** Anycubic Slicer, third-party (PrusaSlicer, OrcaSlicer with profiles)
- **Elegoo printers:** ELEGOO Slicer, CHITUBOX (resin), OrcaSlicer (Centauri Carbon FDM)
- **FlashForge printers:** Orca-Flashforge, OrcaSlicer
- **Snapmaker printers:** Snapmaker Orca, OrcaSlicer
